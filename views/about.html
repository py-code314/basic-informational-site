The Story

Based in New York City, I have spent the last decade capturing the quiet moments between the chaos. My work focuses on the intersection of natural light and human emotion.

Whether I'm shooting a high-fashion editorial or a remote mountain landscape, my goal is the same: to tell a story that feels honest and timeless.

My work has been featured in Vogue, National Geographic, and various independent galleries across Europe.
